Overfeeling

"Sensitivity means-from the latin word-sincere to feel. The more sensitive you are, the more you feel. That means, when bad things happened, you feel them even more. It’s the same sensitive people, because they’re so sensitive, they get hurt so badly. But because of their sensitivity, they’re also the creative ones. So, they are both being hurt in their case and creativity is the extreme sensitivity that they’re born with. And that’s why you see so much pain in very creative people."

(Dr. Gabor Mate)
